Short biography

Ulrich (Uli) Fahrenberg is currently Professor at Université Paris-Saclay. He holds a PhD in mathematics from Aalborg University and has worked at Aalborg University, IRISA Rennes, École polytechnique, and EPITA Rennes.

Fahrenberg works in algebraic topology, concurrency theory, real-time verification, categorical foundations, and general quantitative verification. He has published more than 100 papers in computer science and mathematics. He has been Head of the Automata and applications research group at EPITA and is steering committee co-chair of the RAMiCS and member of the steering committee of the GETCO conferences.
