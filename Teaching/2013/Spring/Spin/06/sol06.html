Sixth and seventh lecture: Some solutions

Friday 23 March 2012, 8:00 to 10:00 and 16:15 to 18:15

  • You can find a suggestion for solving the Boat Problem in boat.pml. This is incomplete, but probably needs only a bit of atomic magic to be made to work.
  • A complete solution to the Frog Puzzle is in frogs.pml. On my machine it can handle up to 16 frogs on each side, and 17 with a pan recompilation. Note the simplicity of the solution: It's running independent move and jump processes for every field of the pond which continuously check whether they can operate.
  • No, there is no solution here for the Rush Hour puzzle.
